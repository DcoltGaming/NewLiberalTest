About us

We are a pressure group that believes in a social market economy, a Norway style Brexit and an increase in spending on public services. We also have three main principles which are pragmatism, individualism and progressive politics. New Liberals has members from across the political spectrum and we hope you will join them!

Progressive


We support a more open, tolerant society that spends more on public services and has a good level of wealth distribution.

The individual


We believe that decisions should be taken as close to the individual level as possible.

Pragmatic


We take ideas based on whether they work rather than trying just trying to place as many sectors in public or private hands as possible.
